Meet Kalee & Our Story

Hi there! I'm Kalee—a homeschooling mama of six, passionate about baking delicious treats right from my home kitchen under cottage food laws. "6 Sprinkles & a Whisk" is truly a small family business, and every single item is made-from-scratch with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of heart. It might involve a little bit of chaos with six kids around, but that just adds to the love!

Thank you for supporting our small family business!
